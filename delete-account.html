How to Delete Your Account

1 Open the GeoMart App
Launch the GeoMart app on your Android device and ensure you're logged into your account.
2 Navigate to Settings
Tap on the "Profile" tab at the bottom of the screen, then tap on "Settings" to access your account settings.
3 Find Account Section
Scroll down to the "Account" section at the bottom of the settings page.
4 Tap "Delete Account"
Tap on the red "Delete Account" button to initiate the deletion process.
5 Confirm Deletion
A confirmation dialog will appear. Type "delete" in the text field to confirm your decision.
6 Complete Deletion
Tap "Delete Account" to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your user profile information (name, username, email, phone number)
  • All your marketplace listings (items for sale)
  • All your service requests
  • Your transaction history and purchase records
  • Your ratings and reviews (both given and received)
  • Your chat messages and conversation history
  • Your saved listings and favorites
  • Your blocked users list
  • Your hidden listings
  • Your reported content history
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Your location data and preferences
  • Your reward points and achievements
  • Your referral history
  • Your daily check-ins and streaks
  • Your feedback and support tickets
  • Your authentication credentials

Data Retention Policy

Immediate Deletion

Most of your data is deleted immediately when you confirm account deletion. This includes:

  • Personal information and profile data
  • User-generated content (listings, messages, reviews)
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Authentication data

Legal Retention Requirements

Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes:

  • Transaction records for tax and accounting purposes (retained for 7 years as required by law)
  • Security logs and audit trails (retained for 2 years for security monitoring)
  • Legal dispute records (retained until resolution plus 3 years)

Note: Any retained data will be anonymized and cannot be linked back to your personal identity.

Selective Data Deletion

If you want to delete specific data types instead of your entire account, you can request selective data deletion by contacting us directly. This allows you to maintain your account while removing specific information.

Data Types You Can Delete Individually:

  • Individual images from your listings
  • Specific marketplace listings
  • Chat conversation history
  • Search and browsing activity
  • Location data and preferences
  • Saved listings and favorites
  • Blocked users list
  • Hidden listings
  • Profile photos and avatars
  • Review and rating history
  • Transaction history (older than 1 year)
  • App usage analytics data

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to delete my account?

Account deletion is processed immediately when you confirm the action. Your data will be removed from our active systems within 24 hours.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent. You would need to create a new account if you want to use GeoMart again.

Will deleting my account cancel any ongoing transactions?

Yes, any pending transactions will be cancelled when your account is deleted. Make sure to complete any important transactions before deleting your account.

What happens to my listings after account deletion?

All your listings will be immediately removed from the marketplace when your account is deleted.

Do I need to delete the app from my device?

Deleting your account only removes your data from our servers. You can choose to keep or uninstall the app from your device.

Can I delete specific data without deleting my entire account?

Yes! You can request selective data deletion by emailing us at risingsoup76@gmail.com. Specify which data types you want to remove, and we'll process your request within 7 business days.

What's the difference between account deletion and selective data deletion?

Account deletion removes your entire account and all associated data permanently. Selective data deletion allows you to remove specific types of data while keeping your account active.

Can I delete individual images from my listings?

Yes, you can request deletion of specific images from your listings through our email support. Provide the listing ID and specify which images you want removed.
